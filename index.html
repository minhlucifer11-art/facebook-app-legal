Facebook Content Automation App

This site provides the legal pages for an application that helps authorized users publish content to Facebook Pages they manage.

What the app does
  • Creates and publishes posts (text, images, videos, links) to authorized Facebook Pages.
  • Optionally schedules posts and stores minimal metadata required to operate the workflow.
  • Uses Facebook Graph API only for the permissions granted by the user.
